# S-NFT Card Levels And Mining Hash Rate

SquidGame S-NFT cards features 5 levels, with level-1 being the lowest and level 5 being the highest. Only Level-1 cards can be purchased directly. Higher level cards can be obtained through upgrades or blind boxes. Currently, every SquidGame S-NFT cards will represent a character from the IP, I AM MT. Typically, higher level cards will corresponds to higher SquidGame and BTC mining hash rate and stronger character character attributes in SquidGame GameFi.
